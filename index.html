About FlowDriver

FlowDriver is a personal SOCKS5 tunnel tool that routes your internet traffic through Google Drive. It is designed to bypass network restrictions and is intended for personal use only.

This project does not collect any personal data from users other than the limited Google Drive folder access that you own.

Main Repository: https://github.com/italy2010/FlowDriver

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

FlowDriver does not collect any personal information such as name, email, or phone number.

The only access required is limited permission to one Google Drive folder that you create yourself. This access is used solely to transfer encrypted tunnel traffic between your client and server.

No analytics, tracking, or cookies are used.
